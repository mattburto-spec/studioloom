📖 What to do

Let's read from the Bible together! If you can read, take turns. If you're younger, just listen carefully. We're reading from James chapter 2, verses 1 to 9, and then verses 14 to 17.

📜 Quick Summary

James says we shouldn't treat rich people better than poor people. God loves everyone the same! And he also says that just saying we believe isn't enough — we need to show our faith by helping people who need it.

Faith + Action = Real Love ❤️

1 Talk about it

Imagine two new kids come to your school. One is wearing really cool brand-name clothes and the other has old, worn-out clothes. How might people treat them differently?

2 Think deeper

Why does God want us to treat everyone the same? Why is it important?

3 Real life

If you saw someone at school sitting alone at lunch, what could you do to show God's love?

🧠 Quick Quiz

In James's story, a rich man and a poor man come to church. What did the people do wrong?
AThey didn't say hello to either person
BThey gave the rich man the best seat and made the poor man stand
CThey asked both of them to leave

AThe Biggest Law — because it's the longest rule
BThe Golden Law — because it's about being golden
CThe Royal Law — because it's the King's most important command

Real Love Action Plan

About 10 minutes
Time for this activity
10:00

✍️ What to do

James chapter 2, verse 17 says "faith by itself, if it is not accompanied by action, is dead." That means we need to show our love, not just talk about it! Use the printed action plan sheet to write down specific things you will do this week.

"Faith by itself, if it is not accompanied by action, is dead."

James 2:17

🤔 What Would You Do?

Your friend tells you they're really sad because their dog is sick. What does "living faith" look like here?
ASay "that's too bad" and keep playing your game
BStop what you're doing, listen to them, and make them a card
CSay "I hope your dog gets better" and walk away

🤔 One More Scenario

There's a kid at your church who always sits alone because they're new and shy. Which response would James be most proud of?
AFeel sorry for them but sit with your own friends
BTell your mum that someone should talk to them
CGo sit with them, introduce yourself, and invite them to join your group

🏠 At Home

Write down one kind thing you can do for your family this week. Be specific! Not just "be nice" — something real, like "help wash up after dinner without being asked."

🏫 At School or With Friends

Write down one way you can show love to someone who feels left out. Think about who might be lonely or need a friend.

🤫 Secret Kindness Mission

Write down something kind you'll do this week without anyone knowing it was you! This is your secret mission from God.

🎨 What to do

Use your printed colouring sheet to colour in the memory verse design. While you colour, try to learn the verse by heart! Say it out loud a few times. Then draw a picture at the bottom of how you can love your neighbour.

"Love your neighbour as yourself."

James 2:8

🧠 Memory Verse Challenge

Try to learn the verse! Here's how:

  • Say it together 3 times while looking at it
  • Cover up one word and say it again
  • Cover up two words and say it again
  • Keep going until you can say the whole thing without looking!
  • Quiz each other — can you say it from memory?

💬 While you colour, talk about this

Who is your "neighbour"? Is it just the person who lives next door, or does it mean something bigger? What's one kind thing you could do for someone this week?
