## China-Australia Trade Intelligence Platform (Umbrella Concept)

*Added 17 March 2026. Revised same day — individual bridge ideas consolidated under one umbrella.*

### The Big Idea

A single platform/brand that scans public data from both Australia and China, cross-references it using AI, and surfaces non-obvious trade opportunities that only someone reading both sides would catch. The individual bridge ideas (supplier verification, e-commerce advising, design consultancy, compliance) are **services under the umbrella**, not separate businesses.

**Core insight:** "Boring" government lists — GACC facility registers, ChAFTA tariff schedules, MOFCOM encouraged imports catalogues, GB/AS standards, CNIPA trademark filings, AusTender procurement boards, provincial tender portals — are only valuable if you can read them from both sides simultaneously. That's a rare skillset, and AI can amplify it massively.

### How it works

1. **Intelligence layer (AI):** Continuously ingests public data sources from AU + CN. Cross-references changes across lists. Surfaces matches (e.g., "tariff on X just hit 0% + MOFCOM lists X as encouraged import + only 3 AU facilities are GACC-registered for X → opportunity"). Gets smarter with every brokered deal — learns real-world details no public list contains (shipping timelines, actual vs. published tariff treatment, which certifications matter).
2. **Newsletter/alerts (audience building):** Weekly free email — "3 things that changed this week in AU-CN trade." Paid tier filters by industry. Builds audience with zero code.
3. **Matchmaking (revenue):** When a signal surfaces, Matt (or eventually a team) brokers the introduction. Helps with compliance paperwork, site visits, cultural adaptation.
4. **Specialist services (high-value add-ons):** The former standalone bridge ideas become service modules — food compliance (CompliMate), packaging/design adaptation, e-commerce channel setup, supplier verification, IP filing guidance.

### Why this is better than 9 separate ideas

- **One brand, one audience.** You build a mailing list once. Every service cross-sells.
- **Compounding intelligence.** Every deal teaches the AI something. A food compliance engagement reveals shipping routes that help the next e-commerce client.
- **Network effects.** The AU exporter you helped with compliance might also need packaging redesign. The CN distributor you connected them with might need IP advice later.
- **Matt doesn't scale; the intelligence layer does.** You personally broker the first 20 deals. The AI remembers everything and eventually the newsletter writes itself.
- **Survives the move home.** When Matt returns to Australia in ~3 years, the CN-side knowledge is baked into the system. He becomes the Australia-based trade advisor with a live intelligence feed from China, not just "a guy who used to live there."

### MVP (zero code required)

1. Pick 3-4 data sources (GACC register, ChAFTA schedule, MOFCOM encouraged imports)
2. Spend one evening scanning for changes
3. Write a short email summarising what changed and what it means
4. Send to 50 people (CommBank friend, AustCham contacts, LinkedIn connections)
5. See who replies. If 3+ people say "tell me more" — you have something.
6. CompliMate becomes the first service module activated under the brand.

### Interactive Trade Map (paid feature concept)

A proof-of-concept was built on 17 March 2026: `docs/ideas/trade-intelligence-map.html`. It shows AU supply nodes on the left, CN demand nodes on the right, with colour-coded connection lines (tariff, demand, regulatory). Hover reveals full context + suggested action. Filter buttons toggle by connection type.

**Why this is a paid feature:** This is the visual version of the intelligence layer. Free newsletter readers get the text summary. Paid subscribers get an interactive, always-updating map where they can explore connections relevant to their industry, hover for detail, filter by signal type, and click through to take action (book a compliance check, request an introduction). The data compounds — every week new signals get added, old ones get resolved. It becomes a live dashboard of AU-CN trade opportunities.

**Tech notes:** Built as a single-file HTML/SVG/JS page. Could be extended with: real-time data ingestion, user-specific filtering (show only my product categories), alert triggers (email me when a new connection appears in dairy), and deal tracking (mark a connection as "pursuing" or "closed"). React version with database-backed nodes would be the production implementation.
