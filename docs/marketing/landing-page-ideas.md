# Landing Page Copy Ideas — "Studio Management" Framing

## Core Insight (March 2026)
A design teacher isn't just teaching — they're a **program manager** running 100+ concurrent mini-projects, each owned by a different student. Different stages, different materials, different timelines. This is **studio management** + pedagogical scaffolding in one tool.

Closest industry terms:
- **Program Management** — managing multiple related projects toward a shared goal
- **Project Portfolio Management (PPM)** — overseeing many projects at different stages
- **Studio Management** — running a studio of practitioners working on individual projects

The twist: the "project managers" (students) are also *learning* how to manage projects.

StudioLoom gives you the oversight of a program manager and your students the structure of a guided design process."

## Positioning Notes
- This framing positions StudioLoom clearly against **LMS tools** (too passive, content delivery) and **generic PM tools** (no pedagogy, not built for classrooms)
- The library, AI unit builder, and safety certs are features that serve the core **orchestration** story
- "100+ projects" is the hook — every design teacher instantly recognizes this problem
- Keep the H1 ("You Teach Design. We Handle the Rest.") — it's catchy and works as the emotional hook. The paragraph below it does the heavy lifting of explaining *what* we handle.
