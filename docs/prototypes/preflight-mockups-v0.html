R-SVG-10

"Read On" text isn't outlined

The Glowforge software needs letter shapes as actual paths, not as text. If the font isn't installed on the Glowforge computer, your text comes out as boxes or blank.

How to fix: In Inkscape: select text → Path → Object to Path. In Illustrator: Type → Create Outlines. Skills Library → Outlining text →

R-SVG-04

Unknown stroke colour — cyan

The Glowforge Pro uses stroke colour to decide what to cut, score, or engrave. The cyan scroll on your design isn't mapped — the Glowforge won't know what to do with it.

Convention on this machine: red = cut · blue = score · black = engrave. Change the cyan scroll to the colour you meant.

Convention source: machine_profiles.operation_color_map JSONB for Glowforge Pro

⚠️

1 should fix

R-SVG-05

Cut stroke is too thick (#1 in preview)

Your cut line is 4 px wide. On most lasers, cut strokes should be ≤0.01 mm (hairline). Thick strokes get interpreted as fill engrave — the rectangle near the top will get burned as a solid block instead of cut out.

ℹ️

Also worth knowing

  • 3 layers detected — 1 cut, 0 score, 1 engrave (the thick stroke), 1 unknown (the cyan scroll)
  • Estimated cut time: 2 min 40 s
  • Material size needed: 180 × 320 mm — fits on Glowforge bed (495 × 279 mm)
  • ✨ R-AI-01 320 mm tall is on the tall side for a bookmark — most are 150–200 mm. Did you mean this size?
