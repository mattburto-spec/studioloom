VALIDATION FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. ALGORITHM CORRECTLY DIFFERENTIATES PEDAGOGICAL APPROACHES
   ✓ Inquiry lessons (Under Pressure) score high on CR + SA
   ✓ Design briefs (Biomimicry, Packaging) score lower on SA (expected)
   ✓ Teacher preparation (craft) is comparable but expressed differently

2. STUDENT AGENCY SCORES REFLECT DESIGN CONSTRAINTS
   • Under Pressure 5.0: Multiple choice points (resource, approach, topic)
   • Packaging 3.8: Design freedom constrained by brief
   • Biomimicry 3.5: Most constrained by brief and timeline
   ✓ Algorithm correctly identifies that briefs reduce agency

3. REAL-WORLD LESSONS REVEAL GAPS IN CURRENT PRACTICE
   ✓ Zero scaffolding (ELL tiers) documented in traditional lessons
   ✓ Zero UDL checkpoint tagging (not yet standard)
   ✓ Differentiation present but not explicit
   ✓ Zero AI rule configuration (expected for pre-AI lessons)
   → These gaps will close once StudioLoom lessons are generated

4. SCORES FEEL RIGHT FOR BLIND EVALUATION
   ✓ 4.4–4.8 reflects that real lessons are incomplete
   ✓ Scores would improve to 6–7+ with:
      - Full 3-tier scaffolding on all text activities
      - Explicit UDL checkpoint tagging
      - Clear student choice moments
      - Per-activity AI rules (phase/tone)
